Butter Chicken

ButterChicken is an Indian recepies which is a great, ever-evolving, cross-continental dish found in Delhi, London,newyork Perth and most points in between. In its purest form, it is yogurt-and-spice-marinated chicken dressed in a velvety red bath comprising butter

Ingredients

list of items

This simple version of the classic Indian chicken dish uses plain yogurt, tomato sauce, ginger, garlic, Indian chili powder, garam masala, dried fenugreek leaves, and, of course, butter, for a tasty main dish served over rice.
